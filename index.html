About Us

At Quantum Systems, we are committed to being the most reliable and customer-friendly laptop sales and service provider in the region.

Sales

Quantum Systems offers a wide selection of laptops from all leading brands, including HP, Dell, Lenovo, Acer, ASUS, Apple, and more. Whether you're a student, professional, gamer, or business owner, we have the perfect laptop to match your needs and budget. We deal in brand-new, certified refurbished, and pre-owned laptops, ensuring that every device is tested for quality and performance. Our knowledgeable team helps customers choose the right configuration—be it for everyday use, high-performance computing, or enterprise solutions—backed by competitive pricing and reliable after-sales support.

Service

Quantum Systems specialize in advanced chip-level laptop repair services, offering precision diagnostics and repair for motherboards and other intricate components. Our certified technicians are equipped with the latest BGA rework stations, oscilloscopes, and diagnostic tools to handle issues at the microelectronic level. Whether it’s a faulty IC, a broken power supply circuit, or a GPU-related problem, we ensure accurate troubleshooting and durable repairs. Our chip-level expertise reduces the need for costly motherboard replacements and extends the life of your devices, making us a trusted choice for high-end technical repairs
